1. Overview

Browser-based app used by all Havellin staff. No installation. Data syncs across devices (iMac ↔ iPad) via Google Sheets. Documents and media auto-upload to Google Drive. Approvals require a manager PIN. Client signatures via DocuSign. Payments via Stripe.

Navigation tabs: New Client · Estimate · Client Estimate · Agreement · Log Hours · Final Invoice · Clients

3. End-to-End Job Workflow

Intake → Estimate → Manager Approval → Send to Client → Client Accepts
→ Send Agreement (DocuSign) → Client Signs → Deposit Auto-Charged (Stripe)
→ Job Active → Document Property → Log Hours → Final Invoice → Complete

4. New Client Intake

All fields marked * are required. Job ID is auto-generated (HVL-YYMM-XXXX). Google Drive folders are created automatically on save — no manual step needed.

Property Types: Apartment · Penthouse · Town Home · Patio Home · Single Family Home · Contemporary Estate · Equestrian Estate · Historic Estate · Waterfront Estate

Estate / Probate jobs require authorized representative (executor, POA, administrator) — name, role, phone, email. Probate additionally requires case number and attorney details.

Drive folder structure created automatically per job:

Photos · Walkthrough Notes · Estimate · Agreement · Change Orders · Asset Documentation

5. Estimate

Tab: Estimate → select job from dropdown

5a. Walkthrough Notes & Voice

During the site visit, use 📝 (notes) and 🎙 (voice) buttons in each room row. Voice transcribes in real time into the notes field. Hit Save Notes — note saves with the estimate and uploads as a .txt file to the job's Walkthrough Notes Drive folder automatically.

📷 Photos are NOT captured during the estimate stage. Photography happens only after job activation.

5b. Room Scoring

Check each room to include it. Set Volume and Complexity on a 1–5 scale:

ScoreVolumeComplexity
1Nearly emptySimple / standard items
3Moderately filledMix of standard and care items
5Completely fullHigh-value / fragile / specialty handling

Check Special Items if the room contains artwork, antiques, or items requiring specialty care. TC and PS hours calculate automatically.

5c. Job Settings

  • Premium Property — TC $175/hr, PS $135/hr (vs. standard $150/$100)
  • PS Count — number of Property Specialists assigned
  • Preferred Customer Discount — percentage off Havellin labor fees only
  • Moving Materials Package — None · Basic · Standard · Premium · Custom
  • Target Completion — appears in estimate and agreement documents

5d. Moving Materials & Third-Party Vendors

Add third-party vendors with estimated costs. A 15% Service Management Fee is automatically calculated on vendor totals. Vendor costs are estimates only — actual invoices billed directly to client at cost.

5e. Home Prep for Sale

Check Include in estimate to activate. Add prep items with estimated vendor costs. 15% SMF calculated per item. TC coordination hours are auto-estimated and added to TC total. Vendor costs can be updated later via Change Order as actual quotes come in.

5f. Notable Collections

Add significant asset collections (artwork, jewelry, antiques, silver, etc.) requiring disposition planning. Set name, estimated value, quantity, and proposed disposition: Auction · Appraise · Consign · Ebay · Replacements.com · Dealer · Other. Collections appear in the client-facing estimate under Notable Collections — Disposition Plan.

5g. Saving

Hit Save & Calculate. Estimate saves locally and syncs to Google Sheets. Editable until approved.

6. Client Estimate & Approval

Tab: Client Estimate → select job. The client-facing estimate document renders here. Review for accuracy. The estimate is informational — no client signature is required or requested.

Approval

Hit Submit for Approval → manager enters PIN → estimate is locked and marked Approved for Release. PDF is unlocked. Estimate cannot be edited after approval — use a Change Order instead.

Sending to Client

Hit Email Estimate → opens pre-drafted email with estimate attached. Send to client for review. Then wait for the client to confirm they want to proceed before taking any further steps.

7. Service Agreement

Tab: Agreement → select job

Only initiate after the client has verbally or by email confirmed they want to proceed. Never send an agreement before the client has accepted the estimate.

Generate & Send

  1. Select job → agreement auto-populates from approved estimate data
  2. Manager enters PIN to approve for sending
  3. Hit Send for Signature → DocuSign delivers agreement to client email

Automated Steps After Sending

  • Client signs via DocuSign → agreement marked signed automatically
  • Stripe deposit invoice (50%) fires automatically upon signature
  • Client pays deposit → job status changes to Active automatically
  • TC receives notification that job is active

Payment Schedule

MilestoneAmountTiming

Third-party vendor invoices are billed separately and directly to client at cost.

Change Orders

If scope changes after estimate approval: Create Change Order → enter description and cost impact → manager approves → sent to client for sign-off via DocuSign.

9. Active Job Documentation

Accessible from Client Dashboard once a job is Active. This is the only stage where photos are captured.

Room Documentation

Each room row shows the walkthrough note from the estimate as a gray italic reference. Capture buttons per room:

  • 📷 — tap to add/review photos. Uploads automatically to Photos Drive subfolder
  • 🎥 — capture video walkthrough footage

Photo naming: HVL-YYMM-XXXX_RoomName_001.jpg

Notable Collections — Asset Documentation

  • 📷 Asset photos — condition and identification photography. Uploads to Asset Documentation subfolder as HVL-YYMM-XXXX_ASSET_CollectionName_001.jpg
  • 📄 Appraisal — upload PDF or image of appraisal document. Saves as HVL-YYMM-XXXX_APPRSL_CollectionName_2026-05-01.pdf

10. Log Hours

Tab: Log Hours → select job. Log each working session: date, team member, role (TC / PS / Specialty), hours, task description. Hours accumulate against the estimate. Log daily — do not batch at end of job.

11. Final Invoice

Tab: Final Invoice → select job. Auto-generates from logged hours vs. estimate. Shows actual TC and PS hours, advance payments applied, balance due, and any approved Change Orders. Manager enters PIN to approve and unlock. Stripe final payment invoice fires automatically to client on approval. Payment due within 7 days.
